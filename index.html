Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Rate My Barber ("we", "us", or "our") operates ratemybarber.org. This page explains what information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you submit a review or add a barber, we collect the name you provide and the content of your review. We do not require you to create an account or provide an email address.

We may automatically collect non-personal information such as browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site through standard web analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through reviews and barber listings is displayed publicly on the site. We use anonymous analytics data to improve the website experience.

Advertising

We may display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

Cookies

Our site may use cookies for analytics and advertising purposes. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

We use Supabase for database storage. Data submitted to our site is stored on Supabase servers. Please review Supabase's Privacy Policy for more information.
